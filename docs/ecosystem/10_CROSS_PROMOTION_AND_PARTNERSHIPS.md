## Part 4: Partnership Model — "Join the Action"

### The Offer

**We're building an ecosystem. You can be part of it.**

Options for others to participate:

| Model | What They Do | What They Get |
|-------|--------------|---------------|
| **Affiliate** | Promote MASTERMIND modules. Use unique link. | Commission on sales (e.g. 10–20%). Gumroad/LS support this. |
| **Module creator** | Build a module (Quality Lens, etc.). We sell it. | Revenue share. Or: They sell, we get attribution. |
| **Integration partner** | Integrate MASTERMIND with their tool (Cursor, VS Code, app). | Cross-promotion. "Official integration." Joint visibility. |
| **Reseller / consultant** | Sell MASTERMIND + setup to teams/enterprises. | Margin on modules. Consulting fees. Our referral. |

## Part 6: Cooperation Principles

**Aligned incentives:** When partners succeed, we succeed. When we succeed, partners succeed.

**Transparent:** Revenue share, commission, terms — clear. No hidden catches.

**Mission-aligned:** Partners who share mutual-flourishing values get priority. We don't partner with extractive or harmful actors.

**Voluntary:** No lock-in. Partners can leave. We can part ways. Respect both ways.
