## 4. When to Deploy

- **Skepticism** — "Another coordination system? Sounds Orwellian." → Show counter-agency, counter-transparent, counter-improve-not-control.
- **Distrust** — "How do I know you're not just another layer of control?" → Trust earned, transparent, free to leave.
- **Dehumanization fear** — "Will I become a cog?" → Human-first, individuals matter, flourishing.
- **Compliance fear** — "Am I forced to follow this?" → Voluntary, question welcome, optional.
- **Lock-in fear** — "Can I escape?" → Free to leave, no cages.
- **Harmony vs. discord** — "Are we just dancing to someone else's tune?" → Harmony, coordinated by choice.

---

## 5. Principles for All MASTERMIND Messaging

1. **Agency first** — Always emphasize choice, optional, voluntary.
2. **Transparency** — Open. Inspectable. No hidden agendas.
3. **Improve, never control** — The verb is "improve." Not "mandate," "enforce," "comply."
4. **Human at center** — Tools serve people. Not the reverse.
5. **Resistance welcomed** — "Improve the system" includes critiquing it.
6. **Exit always possible** — Free core. No lock-in. Leave anytime.
7. **Trust earned** — Through action, documentation, openness. Never demanded.
8. **Flourishing, not grinding** — Mutual benefit. Elevation. Not extraction.
